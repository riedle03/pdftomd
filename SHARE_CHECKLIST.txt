Share this whole folder, not just one file.

Recipient needs:
- Python installed
- Node.js installed
- Microsoft Office installed only if they want .doc/.xls/.ppt support

Recipient steps:
1. Open PowerShell in this folder
2. Run:
   powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File .\setup_windows.ps1
3. Run:
   py .\convert_to_md.py "." --skip-unsupported
